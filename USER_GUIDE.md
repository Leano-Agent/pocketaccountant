# PocketAccountant - User Guide

## Welcome to PocketAccountant! 🦁

PocketAccountant is your personal finance assistant designed specifically for African users. Track expenses, manage budgets, and gain insights into your spending habits - all with support for African currencies and offline capabilities.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
1. Visit the PocketAccountant website
2. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Name**: Your full name
   - **Email**: Your email address
   - **Password**: At least 6 characters
   - **Primary Currency**: Select your main currency (ZAR, NGN, KES, GHS, etc.)
   - **Country** (Optional): Your country of residence
   - **Phone Number** (Optional): For account recovery

### 2. Verify Your Email
- Check your inbox for a verification email
- Click the verification link (if enabled)
- You're now ready to use PocketAccountant!

## Core Features

### 📊 Dashboard
Your financial overview at a glance:
- **Total Balance**: Sum of all your accounts
- **Monthly Spending**: Current month's expenses
- **Budget Progress**: How you're tracking against budgets
- **Recent Transactions**: Latest expenses
- **Quick Actions**: Add expense, view reports, manage budgets

### 💰 Expense Tracking

#### Adding an Expense
1. Click "Add Expense" from any page
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Amount**: How much you spent
   - **Currency**: Select the currency (auto-detects your primary)
   - **Description**: What you bought (e.g., "Groceries at Shoprite")
   - **Category**: Food, Transport, Entertainment, etc.
   - **Date**: When you made the purchase
   - **Receipt** (Optional): Upload a photo of your receipt
   - **Location** (Optional): Where you made the purchase
3. Click "Save Expense"

#### Managing Expenses
- **View All**: See all your expenses in a list
- **Filter**: Filter by date, category, or amount
- **Search**: Find specific expenses by description
- **Edit**: Update expense details
- **Delete**: Remove expenses you no longer need

### 📈 Budget Management

#### Creating a Budget
1. Go to "Budgets" from the sidebar
2. Click "Create New Budget"
3. Set up your budget:
   - **Category**: What you're budgeting for (e.g., Food)
   - **Amount**: Your budget limit
   - **Currency**: Budget currency
   - **Period**: Monthly, Weekly, or Custom
   - **Start/End Date**: Budget duration
4. Click "Save Budget"

#### Tracking Budgets
- **Progress Bars**: Visual indicators of budget usage
- **Alerts**: Notifications when approaching budget limits
- **Adjustments**: Modify budgets as needed
- **History**: View past budget performance

### 🌍 Multi-Currency Support

#### Supported Currencies
- **South Africa**: ZAR (Rand)
- **Nigeria**: NGN (Naira)
- **Kenya**: KES (Shilling)
- **Ghana**: GHS (Cedi)
- **Egypt**: EGP (Pound)
- **West Africa**: XOF (CFA Franc)
- **Central Africa**: XAF (CFA Franc)
- **International**: USD, EUR

#### How It Works
1. **Set Primary Currency**: Choose your main currency during signup
2. **Add Expenses in Any Currency**: Record expenses in their original currency
3. **Automatic Conversion**: All amounts are converted to your primary currency for reporting
4. **Manual Conversion**: Use the currency converter for specific calculations

#### Changing Your Currency
1. Go to "Settings" → "Currency"
2. Select your new primary currency
3. All historical data will be converted automatically

### 📱 Offline Mode

#### How Offline Mode Works
- **Automatic Detection**: App detects when you're offline
- **Local Storage**: All data is saved on your device
- **Sync Queue**: Changes are queued when offline
- **Auto-Sync**: Automatically syncs when back online

#### Using PocketAccountant Offline
1. **Add Expenses**: Works exactly the same as online
2. **View Data**: Access all your stored information
3. **Edit/Delete**: Make changes that will sync later
4. **Sync Status**: Check the sync indicator in the top-right

#### Manual Sync
1. Click the sync icon in the top-right
2. Or go to "Settings" → "Sync"
3. Click "Sync Now" to manually trigger synchronization

### 📱 Mobile Experience

#### PWA (Progressive Web App)
1. **Install on Home Screen**:
   - On Android: Chrome menu → "Add to Home Screen"
   - On iOS: Safari share button → "Add to Home Screen"
2. **Works Like a Native App**:
   - Launch from home screen
   - Works offline
   - Push notifications (coming soon)

#### Mobile-Optimized Features
- **Touch-Friendly**: Large buttons and easy navigation
- **Camera Integration**: Snap receipts directly
- **Location Services**: Auto-tag expense locations
- **Biometric Login**: Fingerprint/Face ID support

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Login Problems
- **Forgot Password**: Use "Reset Password" on login page
- **Account Locked**: Wait 15 minutes or contact support
- **Email Not Verified**: Check spam folder or resend verification

#### Sync Issues
- **Not Syncing**: Check internet connection
- **Data Missing**: Manually trigger sync
- **Duplicate Entries**: Contact support for cleanup

#### App Performance
- **Slow Loading**: Clear browser cache
- **Crashes**: Update to latest version
- **Missing Features**: Check if offline mode is active

#### Currency Conversion
- **Wrong Rates**: Rates update daily, slight variations normal
- **Missing Currency**: Contact us to add new currencies
- **Conversion Errors**: Report to support with details
